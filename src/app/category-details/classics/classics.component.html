Old Fashioned

A timeless whiskey-based cocktail with bitters and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange twist for garnish

Steps

  1. Place the sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass and add bitters.
  2. Add a splash of water and muddle until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Fill the glass with ice and pour in the whiskey.
  4. Stir gently and garnish with an orange twist.

Margarita

A refreshing tequila-based cocktail with lime juice and a salted rim.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • Salt for rim
  • Lime wheel for garnish

Steps

  1. Run a lime wedge around the rim of a glass and dip in salt.
  2. Combine tequila, lime juice, and triple sec in a shaker with ice.
  3. Shake well and strain into the prepared glass.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel and serve immediately.

Martini

A sophisticated gin or vodka-based cocktail with dry vermouth.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin or vodka
  • 1/2 oz dry vermouth
  • Olive or lemon twist for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine gin or vodka and vermouth in a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir well and strain into a chilled martini glass.
  3. Garnish with an olive or lemon twist.

Mojito

A refreshing rum-based cocktail with mint, lime, and soda.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • Soda water
  • Mint sprig and lime wedge for garnish

Steps

  1. Muddle mint leaves and sugar with lime juice in a glass.
  2. Add ice and pour in the rum.
  3. Top with soda water and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wedge.

Manhattan

A smooth whiskey drink with rich, sweet notes.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz rye whiskey
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes of Angostura bitters
  • Cherry for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, and bitters in a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir until well chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with a cherry.

Daiquiri

A classic rum cocktail with a tart lime flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 0.75 oz simple syrup

Steps

  1. Combine rum, lime juice, and simple syrup in a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well until chilled.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with a lime wheel, if desired.
