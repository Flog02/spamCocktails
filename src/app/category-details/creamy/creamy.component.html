Old Fashioned

A timeless whiskey-based cocktail with bitters and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange twist for garnish

Steps

  1. Place the sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass and add bitters.
  2. Add a splash of water and muddle until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Fill the glass with ice and pour in the whiskey.
  4. Stir gently and garnish with an orange twist.
