Black and Tan

A classic layered beer cocktail made with a pale ale and a dark stout.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pint pale ale (such as Bass)
  • 1/2 pint dark stout (such as Guinness)

Steps

  1. Pour the pale ale into a pint glass halfway.
  2. Slowly pour the dark stout over a spoon to layer it on top.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy the beautiful layers.

Michelada

A spicy and tangy Mexican beer cocktail perfect for a hot day.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz lager (such as Corona)
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 dash hot sauce
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • Pinch of salt
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime wedge and salt for rim

Steps

  1. Rim a glass with salt by rubbing the lime wedge around the edge.
  2. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  3. Add lime juice, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Pour the beer into the glass and gently stir.
  5. Garnish with a lime wedge and serve immediately.

Snakebite

A sharp mix of lager and cider with a distinctive taste.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pint lager
  • 1/2 pint cider

Steps

  1. Pour the lager into a glass halfway.
  2. Slowly add the cider to fill the rest of the glass.
  3. Mix gently and serve chilled.

Radler

A refreshing mix of beer and lemonade for a light, citrusy drink.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pint beer (lager or pilsner)
  • 1/2 pint lemonade

Steps

  1. Pour the beer into a glass halfway.
  2. Top it up with lemonade.
  3. Stir gently and serve chilled with a lemon slice.
